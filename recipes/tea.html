Tea

This is how I make my tea. The primary ingredients are Tetley Orange Pekoe Tea, 2% milk and granulated sugar.

Imgredients:

  • Tetley orange pekoe teabag
  • 2% milk
  • Sugar

Steps:

  1. Boil water and put tea in cup for approximately 4 minutes
  2. Remove teabag and add sugar and milk
