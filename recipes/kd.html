Kraft Dinner

This is plain old Kraft Dinner. The primary ingredients pasta, powdered cheese, butter and milk.

Ingredients:
  • Kraft Dinner pasta
  • Kraft Dinner powdered cheese
  • Butter
  • Milk
Steps:
  1. Boil pasta for 7 minutes, then strain
  2. Melt butter into pasta, then mix in cheese until it melts
  3. Add milk, stir, then serve
